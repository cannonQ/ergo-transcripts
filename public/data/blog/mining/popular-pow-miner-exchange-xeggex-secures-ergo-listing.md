# Popular PoW miner exchange, Xeggex, secures Ergo listing

> **Published:** 2024-02-21 | **Author:** Ergo Platform | **Tag:** Mining, Announcements | **Type:** news

*The latest Ergo Raffle has concluded, and we are happy to announce that Xeggex (pronounced Zeg-Ex) have secured enough ERG tokens from 59 of our community members to have ERG listed on their exchange! *

**Related concepts:** ErgoRaffle, Native tokens

---

## Why is this significant?

[Xeggex](https://xeggex.com/) is a popular cryptocurrency exchange amongst the Proof-of-Work mining community, making the listing of ERG tokens on the exchange a perfect fit. As a Proof-of-Work model, Ergo’s vision, community, and ideals are fully aligned with Xeggex, and we expect Ergo’s reception on Xeggex to be very well-received.

To achieve this, our community was able to raise 2,229 ERG tokens equalling a total of $3,500 USD at the time of this announcement! The amount raised will be directly used to help provide liquidity to the ERG/USDT and ERG/BTC pairs [which are now available](https://xeggex.com/markets) on the exchange!

This is a welcome addition to Ergo’s already [wide list of exchanges](https://ergoplatform.org/en/get-erg/#Exchanges), which include the likes of KuCoin, Gate.io, and BitPanda. We hope this listing will hit close to home for many of you who have been part of Ergo’s community for a long time, and we’re extremely excited to be able to give our community with even more liquidity-provision opportunities.
