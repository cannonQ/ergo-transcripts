# Ergo Foundation Announces Grant to Support CyberVerse Marketing and Ecosystem Growth

> **Published:** 2024-09-16 | **Author:** Ergo Platform | **Tag:** Community, DAOs | **Type:** news

**Related concepts:** Cyberverse, Ergo Foundation

---

The Ergo Foundation is thrilled to announce the latest step in [its initiative to foster the growth of the Ergo Ecosystem and community-driven projects](https://ergoplatform.org/en/blog/Ergo-Foundation-Announces-New-Initiative-to-Accelerate-Ecosystem-Growth/). As part of this effort, the Foundation has approved a **$12,000 grant** to support **CyberVerse**, an innovative gaming project on the Ergo blockchain. This funding will directly contribute to marketing efforts, community-building initiatives, and long-term engagement within the ecosystem.

## Empowering CyberVerse and Ergo Ecosystem Growth

The CyberVerse grant aims to expand the game's reach and drive user adoption of the Ergo blockchain by supporting key marketing initiatives. These include:
- **Partnering with Content Creators**: Supporting influencers and content creators to showcase CyberVerse across social media platforms, generating organic interest, and attracting new players to the game and the Ergo ecosystem.
- **Community Competitions**: Hosting engaging competitions to foster user-generated content, spark creativity, and incentivize player involvement.
- **Virtual Community Events**: Organizing regular events, such as live streams and tournaments to strengthen community ties and maintain high engagement levels.
- **Professional Marketing Content**: Producing high-quality promotional materials to enhance CyberVerse's visibility and appeal to a broader audience.

This grant not only supports the growth of CyberVerse but also plays a crucial role in introducing new users to the Ergo blockchain, aligning with our mission to foster adoption through innovative and community-driven projects.

## Transparent Reporting and Community Updates

To ensure accountability and transparency, the CyberVerse team will provide regular monthly updates on how the grant funds are being utilized. These updates will be shared with the community, allowing everyone to track the progress and impact of this initiative.

## Looking Ahead

As we continue to support innovative projects like CyberVerse, we are excited about the future of the Ergo ecosystem. By empowering projects that create value and attract users, we believe that Ergo is well-positioned for sustainable growth.

Stay tuned for more updates on CyberVerse’s progress!
