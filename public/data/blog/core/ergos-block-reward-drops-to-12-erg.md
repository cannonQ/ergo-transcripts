# Ergo Block Reward Drops to 12 ERG — 6 July 2025

Ergo has just completed it's most recent step in it's scheduled emission plan.  
The base reward fell from **15 ERG** to **12 ERG**, exactly three months after the previous reduction.  
Blocks arrive every 120 seconds, so about 720 new blocks appear each day.

---

## What the Change Means

* **Daily inflation:** The chain now releases **8 640 ERG** per day. Yesterday’s figure was 10 800 ERG.  
* **Quarterly pattern:** The reward falls by 3 ERG every three months until it reaches 3 ERG.  
* **Long-term cap:** Total supply still aims for 97 739 925 ERG. The schedule remains automatic and transparent.

## Looking Ahead

The next reduction will cut the block reward to 9 ERG on or around 4 October 2025. Two further steps will bring the chain to its 3 ERG floor by April 2026.  
This smooth staircase limits market shocks while miners, traders, and developers adjust to a gradually tighter supply.
