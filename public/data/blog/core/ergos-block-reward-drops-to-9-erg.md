# Ergo Block Reward Drops to 9 ERG — 4 Oct 2025

Ergo has just completed it's most recent step in it's scheduled emission plan.  
The base reward fell from **9 ERG** to **6 ERG**, exactly three months after the previous reduction.  
Blocks arrive every 120 seconds, so about 720 new blocks appear each day.

## Looking Ahead

The next reduction will lower the reward to 6 ERG in about 80 days, moving toward the 3 ERG final floor expected by April 2026.
This predictable, gradual descent helps the market and miners adapt smoothly to a tightening emission curve while preserving long-term sustainability.
