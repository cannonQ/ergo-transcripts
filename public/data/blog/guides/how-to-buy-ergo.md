# Ergo (ERG) Buying Guide

Ergo (ERG) is available on various centralized and decentralized exchanges, making it accessible for users around the world. This guide will walk you through the process of purchasing ERG using different exchanges and payment methods, as well as providing resources to help you learn more about the project.

## Overview

To get started, you'll need to buy a stablecoin, such as USDT, on your preferred exchange. Once you have your stablecoin, transfer it to an exchange that supports ERG trading pairs. Afterward, you can trade your stablecoin for ERG tokens. We have compiled a list of exchanges that support ERG trading pairs, as well as additional information about each exchange.

Remember to always verify the exchange's website for the most accurate and up-to-date information.

## Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Buy USDT with Fiat

Purchase USDT (or another stablecoin) using your preferred fiat currency on your current exchange or on-ramp.

### 2. Transfer USDT

Send your USDT to an exchange that supports ERG trading pairs, such as CoinEx, KuCoin, or Gate.io. If possible, use the TRC (Tron) network for lower fees.

### 3. Buy ERG

Trade your USDT for ERG tokens on the exchange.

### Using Coinbase

If you're using Coinbase as your on-ramp, note that they don't support USDT on the Tron network. Instead, you can send ETH, ADA, XLM, or another low-fee alternative.
