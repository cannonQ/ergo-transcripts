# Ergo Wallet Update: Ergo Fully Integrated with Ledger

> **Published:** 2025-04-22 | **Author:** Ergo Platform | **Tag:** Tooling | **Type:** news

*Ergo Fully Integrated with Ledger*

**Related concepts:** Ergo Foundation, Native tokens, Nautilus

---

For many years, the Ergo Foundation and community worked very hard to encourage Ledger Hardware wallets to provide support for the Ergo coin and its native assets. The goal was to have an industry endorsed hardware wallet provide secure and user-friendly access to Ergo holders. It has been a long road to get here, but with the support of the Ergo Foundation and the tireless efforts of some Ergo’s most prominent developers, Ergonauts can now fully utilize Ledger with their Ergo wallets.

The first hurdle towards Ledger integration was completed by Captain Nemo of Nautilus Wallet. They undertook laying the framework by completing the code for Ledger usage. Once completed however, the Ergo app was only available via dev mode on the Ledger Live Application.

The Ergo Foundation recently spent funds to have the code audited by a third party auditing firm, and now for the first time ever, Ergo is fully available for download on the Ledger Live Application. Users are no longer required to adjust the application settings in order to access dev mode. The Ergo App is readily available on Ledger Live along with all other asset application listings. Simply install [Ledger Live](https://support.ledger.com/article/6892461437597-zd?redirect=false) on your computer, connect your Ledger device to the computer, and search for Ergo in the list of applications.

Congratulations to all those who worked to make this happen! As we work to make Ergo a blockchain for everyone, this is a monumental industry achievement for Ergo and the community on the cryptocurrency mainstage.
