# Ergo Foundation Announces ErgoHack V: Mining and Minting


> **Published:** 2022-09-01 | **Author:** Ergo Platform | **Tag:** Events, Mining | **Type:** news

*ErgoHack V is Co-Presented by the Ergo Foundation, Flux, Alephium and the Djed Alliance*

**Related concepts:** ERGOHACK, Ergo Foundation, Layer scaling, SigmaUSD, Storage rent

---

This fall, the Ergo Foundation will host the fifth iteration of the highly successful ErgoHack series. This latest hackathon will take place from October 3-20, 2022, and it will be co-presented with [Flux](https://runonflux.io/), [Alephium](https://alephium.org/) and the [Djed Alliance](https://www.djed.one/). With the much anticipated Ethereum Merge coming this fall, this ErgoHack aims to inspire developers and entrepreneurs to explore projects as they relate to mining and minting with the potential for cross-chain development and deployment (for example, consider the ways your project could take advantage of the tools on these other blockchains). Please note that although the theme of the hackathon deals with mining and minting, any and all project proposals are welcome for submission.

As a Layer 1 blockchain, Ergo is one of the most active platforms in terms of developer activity. Ergo hackathons have become an identifying feature of the platform over the last year, and previous ErgoHacks have yielded some of the most used dApps currently in the ecosystem. With the upcoming Ethereum Merge, there will be countless miners looking for a new crypto to mine, and Ergo is poised to welcome a great deal of those miners. The Ergo Platform is optimally situated to offer miners several technological innovations in the Proof of Work mining industry (ie. Storage Rent, governance responsibilities, etc). 

With an eye to the future of Proof of Work mining, ErgoHack V: Mining and Minting will primarily focus on creating dApps, tools, and infrastructure to further attract miners that will be looking for a new crypto to mine after the Ethereum Merge. Those who are interested in participating in this exciting opportunity are encouraged to visit the [ErgoHack website](https://ergohack.io/) for details on how to submit an application. The ErgoHack V prize pool is also the largest to date! Prizes include:

**1st Prize - 10k SigUSD
2nd Prize - 6k SigUSD
3rd Prize - 2k SigUSD**

Additional prizes from co-presenters will include a variable number of DACAU (Djed Alliance Contribution Accounting Unit), 20k ALPH, and more!

Ergo is setting the new standard in the evolution of Proof of Work blockchains. Together with our partners, participants are invited to help us build the future of blockchain and to explore the potential for cross-chain deployment. Are you ready to help build the future? Visit the [Ergo Platform website](https://ergoplatform.org/en/) for more information on Ergo and get your [ErgoHack application](https://ergohack.io/) started today!
