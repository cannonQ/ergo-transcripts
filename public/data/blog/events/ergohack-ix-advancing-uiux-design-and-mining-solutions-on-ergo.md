# ErgoHack IX: Advancing UI/UX Design and Mining Solutions on Ergo

> **Published:** 2024-09-24 | **Author:** Ergo Platform | **Tag:** Events | **Type:** news

**Related concepts:** ERGOHACK, Ergo Foundation, Rosen Bridge

---

It’s almost time for the next Ergo Platform hackathon! ErgoHacks have become a hallmark of the ecosystem, and they have played an integral role in the growth and development of the blockchain. 

With the deployment of numerous dApps, lending protocols, and Rosen Bridge, Ergo is poised to offer much to the world of decentralized finance. In an effort to continue building a blockchain for everyone, the next ErgoHack will encourage developers to build new mining solutions and enhance the user experience through UI/UX.

## ErgoHack IX Details

ErgoHack IX will take place from Oct. 25-31, 2024 on Ergo’s Discord. Participants who have experience as UI/UX designers, front-end developers, and/or blockchain developers are all encouraged to apply. If you are a mining enthusiast or a student/hobbyist in blockchain tech, you are also welcome to submit an application. 

PRIZES TBC

Special Recognition prizes will be awarded to projects with:
- the best UI/UX design
- most innovative mining solution
- a community choice award

## Participant Objectives and Judging Criteria

All participants are encouraged to build solutions that meet the following objectives:

- **Promote UI/UX Excellence**: The judging committee would like to see the building of user-friendly interfaces for Ergo applications.
- **Innovative Mining Solutions**: How can the mining process be made more efficient and accessible?
- **Collaborative Spirit**: Participants are encouraged to find ways to bring the work of multiple developers and dApp designers together to enhance the overall Ergo ecosystem experience.
- **Education**: There is a need for workshops on the best way to design and implement UI/UX and mining solutions.
- **Community Engagement**: Create a meaningful impact on the Ergo ecosystem community. Projects should look to harness the power of the existing community while also creating ways to engage new participants. 

At the end of ErgoHack IX, the judging committee will award the prizes based on the following criteria:

- User Experience
- Innovation
- Technical Execution
- Presentation
- Community Engagement

If you are interested in participating, please visit the [ErgoHack website](https://ergohack.io/) and fill out an application form there. You can also interact with the Ergo Foundation on [X](https://x.com/ergo_platform?ref_src=twsrc%5Egoogle%7Ctwcamp%5Eserp%7Ctwgr%5Eauthor), [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/ergo-platform-668903786361651200), and [Telegram](https://t.me/ergoplatform) if you have any questions about the application process.
