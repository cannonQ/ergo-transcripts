# ErgoHACK X: Artificial Intelligence on the Ergo Blockchain

> **Published:** 2025-04-10 | **Author:** Ergo Platform | **Tag:** Events | **Type:** blog

**Related concepts:** ERGOHACK, Ergo Foundation, Registers, eUTXO

---

## Celebrating a Decade of Decentralized Innovation
Join the 10th anniversary ErgoHACK and be at the forefront of the AI revolution on the Ergo blockchain! Unleash your creativity, collaborate with fellow innovators, and build the future of decentralized AI.

## About ErgoHack
ErgoHACK, the flagship community hackathon, celebrates its tenth anniversary! This milestone event is a defining feature of the Ergo ecosystem, offering developers and entrepreneurs an incredible opportunity to propose, workshop, and build decentralized applications (dApps) on the Ergo Platform.

## Theme: Artificial Intelligence (AI) on the Ergo Blockchain
For this special anniversary ErgoHACK, explore the limitless potential of AI on the Ergo blockchain. Ergo's unique eUTXO model provides a robust foundation for AI innovation. Its simple contract interactions, P2P financial tools, and the ability to leverage Ergo’s contract evolution create a fertile ground for developing diverse AI applications. These features allow for complex AI integrations while maintaining security and transparency, making Ergo an ideal platform for building trustless and intelligent systems.

## Possibilities for AI on Ergo
- Ergo's architecture opens up a wide range of possibilities for AI applications, including:
- Enhanced security through AI-driven smart contract auditing.
- Innovative development tools powered by AI to streamline the Ergo ecosystem.
- Platforms for decentralized AI education and research.
- Advanced financial tools and services leveraging AI capabilities.
- AI solutions for social good and community empowerment.
- Creative and artistic applications that push the boundaries of AI on blockchain.

## Inspiration Topics
Participants are encouraged to think broadly and creatively, considering AI applications in diverse areas such as:
- AI-Driven Smart Contract Auditing and Security
- AI-Powered Development Tools for the Ergo Ecosystem
- Decentralized AI Education and Research Platforms
- AI-Enhanced Financial Tools and Services
- AI for Social Good and Community Empowerment
- Creative and Artistic Applications of AI on Ergo

## Important Information
### Timeframe: **25 May 2025 - 1 June 2025**
### Judging Criteria
- Innovation and originality
- Technical execution and code quality
- User experience and design
- Potential impact on the Ergo ecosystem
- Alignment with the theme of “Artificial Intelligence on the Ergo Blockchain”

At the end of the adjudication process, the judges select three winners, and they will each receive a portion of the prize pool. Winners will earn a share of 9K$ from Ergo Foundation.

🥈2nd Prize - $2,700 in ERGs
🥉3rd Prize - $1,800 in ERGs
