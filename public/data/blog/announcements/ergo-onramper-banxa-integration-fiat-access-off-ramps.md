# Ergo Onramper & Banxa Integration: Fiat Access & Off-Ramps

> **Published:** 2025-06-05 | **Author:** Ergo Platform | **Tag:** Announcements | **Type:** news

**Related concepts:** Ergo Foundation, ErgoRaffle, Native tokens, Nautilus

---

**The Purpose of This Raffle:**
[This raffle](https://ergoraffle.com/raffle/show/ccf1fb1949cfbba4a00e9134b8325eaa1f52afb7bc8e6255c62b46da90d8445f) is to crowdfund the integration of **Onramper and Banxa** into the Ergo ecosystem. Most importantly, this will enable easy US dollar (USD) fiat **on-ramps (to purchase ERG) and off-ramps (to convert ERG back to fiat)**, significantly boosting Ergo's accessibility and utility.

**The Opportunity for Ergo:**
Direct fiat on and off-ramps are a critical piece of infrastructure. By integrating with Onramper (a leading fiat gateway aggregator) and Banxa (a major global payment solutions provider), we can drastically lower the barrier to entry and simplify the experience for everyone wanting to **buy, sell, and use ERG**.

This integration aims to provide extensive global coverage, giving access to most places. Key exceptions where services may be restricted include Hawaii, Louisiana, and New York within the US, mainland China, and approximately 30 other smaller countries due to specific regulatory landscapes. While the UK will be supported via Banxa, it won't be available through Onramper because of FCA rules. However, for most users around the world, this will still enable a more seamless experience when interacting with the Ergo ecosystem using traditional currencies.

- [What countries are supported by Banxa](https://support.banxa.com/en/support/solutions/articles/44002216505-what-countries-are-supported-by-banxa-)
- [Onramper Coverage](https://www.onramper.com/coverage)

Additionally, the Onramper widget can be integrated directly into ecosystem tooling like Nautilus to enable effortless onboarding for new users. 

**Focus of Community Funds & Ergo Foundation Commitment:**

The $20,000 USD raised from this community raffle will be directly allocated to cover essential costs for the Onramper and Banxa integration, including but not limited to:

* Initial platform integration fees and setup charges for both on-ramp and off-ramp functionalities at Banxa.
* Integration fees with Onramper.
* Technical development and any other work required for seamless ERG support.

The **Ergo Foundation is substantially co-funding this entire initiative**. They are committed to covering all additional costs beyond the community's $20,000 contribution, ensuring the full scope of the project (including robust on-ramping and off-ramping) is realized and that these crucial fiat gateways are sustainably integrated and supported for the long term. Your contribution is a key part of this larger, collaborative effort to elevate Ergo!
