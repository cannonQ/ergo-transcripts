# Huobi Officially Lists Ergo


> **Published:** 2022-09-07 | **Author:** Ergo Platform | **Tag:** Announcements | **Type:** news

*For many months now, the Ergo community has been waiting with excitement and anticipation for news on a Tier 1 exchange listing. As of today, the Ergo Foundation is pleased to announce that Huobi cryptocurrency exchange has officially listed Ergo for trading. Per their announcement, Huobi will launch Ergo on their exchange with the following schedule:*

**Related concepts:** Ergo Foundation, Native tokens

---

September 7 - Huobi will allow Ergo deposits beginning at 13:00 UTC
September 9 - Huobi will begin Ergo withdrawals at 13:00 UTC

This listing marks an important step in the Ergo Foundation’s work to promote the Ergo Platform. The Huobi listing also greatly increases access to the ERG token, including to Huobi’s already extensive customer base. 

For more information on Huobi, and to start trading on this exchange, please visit the [Huobi website](https://www.huobi.com/en-us/).
