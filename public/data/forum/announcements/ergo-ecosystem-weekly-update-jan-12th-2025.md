### Post #1 — @kushti (2025-01-13)

* Core and ecosystem developers are deciding on current status and desiderata for 2025. Join discussions over Telegram: [Telegram: Contact @ErgoDevelopers](https://t.me/ErgoDevelopers) or #development channel in Ergo discord
* A contract with Ledger integration code audit party is signed!
* For Keystone integration, parsing and signing mostly done, and pgr456 now working on UI integration
* Rosen bridge: Doge-related changes in rosen-chain (a Guard dependency) are under final review. Also, Binance tests are progressing, with invalid scenarios being tested to ensure that events are correctly rejected and do not create commitments by watchers. And the team is integrating newly refactored packages for Ergo multi-sig and transaction agreement in Guard to improve and accelerate the signing process.
* Ergo in 2025 video by kushti and Joe released <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9QneAIjfKhM>
