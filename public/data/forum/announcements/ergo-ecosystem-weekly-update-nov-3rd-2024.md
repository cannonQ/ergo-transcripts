### Post #1 — @kushti (2024-11-04)

* ErgoHack IX finished, and 9 nice projects got submitted: Community Liquidity Bootstrap, Stable Benefaction Platform, Last Byte Bar, OnErgo, Satergo Offline Vault, Minotaur Wallet improvements, OneStopShop, Cup of Sugar, 3D Explorer
* Paideia Mainnet is deployed on chain
* Sigmanauts DAO created
* Ergo node 5.1.0 finalized and under review
* Ledger Stax/Flex support code is now being reviewed by the Ledger team [Update to the new SDK, UX updates, bugfixes by ypopovych · Pull Request #11 · LedgerHQ/app-ergo · GitHub](https://github.com/LedgerHQ/app-ergo/pull/11)
* rsERG/ETH pool on Uniswap continues to grow, and now some mining pools, such as WoolyPooly [x.com](https://x.com/Woolypooly_com/status/1851884490861240605) , proposing miners to use idle ETH there!
