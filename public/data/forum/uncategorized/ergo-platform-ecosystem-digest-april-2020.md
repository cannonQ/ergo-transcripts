* Building Ergo series continues: Storage rent [ergoplatform.org/en/blog/2020\_04\_21\_ergo\_positioning/](http://ergoplatform.org/en/blog/2020_04_21_ergo_positioning/) Oracles <https://ergoplatform.org/en/blog/2020_04_04_building_ergo_oracles/> Atomic Swaps <https://ergoplatform.org/en/blog/2020_04_24_atomic_swaps/>

* First stablecoin experiment on top of Ergo Platform - one-way convertible stablecoin tokens being bought from the holding contract [Tethering a token to USD using the rate oracle](https://www.ergoforum.org/t/tethering-a-token-to-usd-using-the-rate-oracle/118/9)
* Ergo is pioneering the declarative model as a better and safer alternative to the now popular imperative model of smart contracts. Introducing FlowCards [ergoplatform.org/en/blog/2020\_04\_29\_flow\_cards/](http://ergoplatform.org/en/blog/2020_04_29_flow_cards/)
