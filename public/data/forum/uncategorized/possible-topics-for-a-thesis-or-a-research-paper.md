### Post #1 — @kushti (2024-10-03)

Lets provide useful examples of possible topics for a thesis or a research papers here:

Computer Science:

* Eclipse attacks on Ergo nodes and counter-measures (research on Bitcoin Core clients: <https://www.usenix.org/conference/usenixsecurity15/technical-sessions/presentation/heilman> etc)
* Looking for DoS attacks possibilities on Ergo nodes and counter-measures
* Improving blacklisting rules (introduce graylists maybe) for P2P layer
* Lazy loading of authenticated state ([Load UTXO set's AVL+ tree lazily · Issue #1707 · ergoplatform/ergo · GitHub](https://github.com/ergoplatform/ergo/issues/1707)), finding most performant strategies and parameters for it

Economics / monetary:

* Implement LETS (local exchange trading system, collateralized or not), [Local Exchange Trading Systems - ErgoDocs](https://docs.ergoplatform.com/uses/lets/) (do backend and UI, test within a group of people)
* Implement different monetary systems and applications on top of ChainCash [GitHub - ChainCashLabs/chaincash: ChainCash - elastic peer-to-peer money collectively backed by blockchain assets and trust](https://github.com/ChainCashLabs/chaincash)

Please feel free to add.
