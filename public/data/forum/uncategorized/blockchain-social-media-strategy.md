## Nostr

> [Nostr](https://github.com/nostr-protocol/nostr) is a decentralized network based on cryptographic keypairs and that is not peer-to-peer; it is super simple and scalable and therefore has a chance of working. [Read more about the protocol.](https://github.com/nostr-protocol/nostr)

Launched in December, seems to be [picking up some steam](https://nostr.band/stats.html)

Easiest place to get started is via the [iris web client](https://iris.to)

Community is small at the moment but growing, and may be a valuable channel to get relevant news to Bitcoin maxis. (Cypherpunk, Rosen Bridge, etc).

* There is a Reddit clone in development at [nvote.co](https://nvote.co/). However, it seems to be mostly inactive at the moment.
* [nostr.directory](https://nostr.directory/) lets you find your Twitter followers on Nostr.
