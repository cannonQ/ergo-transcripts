Logging back into my old mastodon account, there’s a decent amount of [activity and users building up](https://observablehq.com/@simonw/mastodon-users-and-statuses-over-time).

Many of them are software developers, business leaders or other professionals who are still engageable without the noise of the hundreds of millions of users on Twitter drowning any good-faith discussion out generally.

There are also lots of crypto critics on Mastodon. Not one to shy away from a debate, it’s an easy way to engage with prominent critics, and learn from the mistakes of other cryptocurrencies. A list is given below.

## Getting Started

* [Sign up for an account](https://joinmastodon.org/servers), you can change the drop-downs here to select ‘Public Organization’ legal structure and ‘Instant’ sign-up speed.
* [moa.party](https://moa.party) lets you bridge your Twitter<>Mastodon easily. I recommend not bridging RTs/QTs
* [fedi.directory](https://fedi.directory/) has some tips for getting started on Mastodon, as well as some categorised accounts to follow.

The search on mastodon is purposefully bad (for privacy). Best way to find stuff is to add hashtags to your Home feed, and then jump on any related discussions that pop up!

### Post #9 — @glasgowm (2024-11-18)

Bluesky has been growing pretty quickly since the US election.

* [Bluesky Might End Up Defeating Twitter Once and for All](https://slate.com/technology/2024/11/bluesky-app-twitter-exodus-x-elon-musk.html)
* [The Great Migration to Bluesky Gives Me Hope for the Future of the Internet](https://www.404media.co/the-great-migration-to-bluesky-gives-me-hope-for-the-future-of-the-internet/)

And will likely pass the 20M user mark sometime today. ~5M of those have been in the past week. You can follow & see historical stats here.

* [Bluesky Stats](https://vqv.app/stats/)
* [Atlas - Engagement-Based Social Graph for Bluesky by Jaz (jaz.bsky.social)](https://bsky.jazco.dev/stats)

Bluesky is an open-source microblogging platform powered by the AT Protocol. It functions pretty similar to Twitter so there isn’t much of a learning curve, just sign up at [bsky.app](https://bsky.app/).

* For a mobile app, the default one is pretty good and similar to Twitter, there’s also [openvibe.social/](https://openvibe.social/) that does Mastodon, Bluesky, Nostr, Threads & more from one app.
* There’s an awesome-bluesky page with all [clearsky](https://github.com/fishttp/awesome-bluesky) links and things like [skyfeed.app](https://skyfeed.app/) which are also popular (Clearsky= bluesky things outside the federated network hosted on the regular web)
* cafe has kindly made up an [Ergo Starter Pack](https://bsky.app/starter-pack/cafebedouin.bsky.social/3lb4kbaxvh52l), this is a quick way to follow all ergonauts who’ve registered on bluesky. (Reply to [this](https://bsky.app/profile/cafebedouin.bsky.social/post/3lb4kdnu47f2d) post once registered to be added)
* I’ve made a [‘cryptosky’ feed](https://bsky.app/profile/glasgow.bsky.social/feed/aaaltsdb6p62e), this tracks a bunch of keywords and accounts involved in crypto or related topics and filters out anyone I’ve added to the [‘Twats’ list](https://bsky.app/profile/glasgow.bsky.social/lists/3kkzcppwri62f). **There aren’t many crypto feeds on bluesky yet so please add a like to this one so we can bump it up to the top!**.

Here are some other related feeds to follow

* [₿sky by ‪@bitcoin.bsky.social‬](https://bsky.app/profile/bitcoin.bsky.social/feed/aaab3fy25acf2)
* [Crypto by ‪@respectfulmemes.bsky.social‬](https://bsky.app/profile/respectfulmemes.bsky.social/feed/aaacg5mlmmibi)
* [Cryptocurrency by ‪@ppl.bsky.social‬](https://bsky.app/profile/ppl.bsky.social/feed/aaallnp65nooy)
* And you can view all starter packs [here](https://blueskydirectory.com/starter-packs/all?q=)

The vibe is still heavily anti-crypto mostly. However because of the way bluesky works, the people who aren’t interested in seeing crypto posts will subscribe to a blocklist and not have to be subjected to it if they don’t want to be. Most of the people who are anti-crypto are simply that way because of all the scams, rugs and BS in the space. So you can’t really blame them. They haven’t heard of Ergo.

In just a few hours of messing around with it last night I have several good discussions about LETS with non-crypto people, and discussions with Bitcoin maxis on why they should be maxis. Some big influencers have also started moving over and they’re much easier to get through to without being drowned out by the noise on Twitter.

So there are good discussions to be had, and I think it’s probably the best way to do some ‘debubbling’ from Twitter which has been stagnant for a while. Even very successful posts rarely net you new followers, and we seem to mostly be talking to each other. So please sign up and start chatting!
