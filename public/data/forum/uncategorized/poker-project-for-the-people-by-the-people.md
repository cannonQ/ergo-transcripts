### Post #1 — @WuWei (2024-10-17)

1. We need to create a poker site and an application like poker stars, with the ability to play with stable coins.
2. We launch a new token on ergo, the owners of which will receive a part of the profit (part of all rakes) of the site/app proportionally to the number of ERGOPOKER (EPOK) tokens. For example, the owner of 10% of all tokens receives 10% of all profits.
3. The token also carries a management function. For example: token owners determine the rake size.
4. To receive profit/management, tokens must be blocked in staking, which leads to a decrease in the token supply in circulation.
5. Thus, each token owner actually becomes the owner of the project and is interested in advertising the project and inviting their friends there.
6. It is necessary to conduct the largest possible IDO so that as many people as possible become owners of ERGOPOKER (EPOK), for this IDO should be held on different platforms. (coinlist, ergopad and others). At the same time, the development team does not have the right to sell their tokens, their tokens are blocked in staking, they receive a salary from the profit and support the project.
7. Pitch this idea to Daniel Negreanu ))
8. Make ergo great again! Jokes aside, what do you think?
