There will be AMA with Cypra team (NCrashed and VoidLizard) on Monday, 18:00 UTC! on Ergo telegram [https://t.me/ergoplatform](https://t.co/MKUbpUEJDs?amp=1)

Cypra is a multi currency light wallet based on client side BIP158 filters for desktop and mobile platforms.

The major focus is privacy. The whole storage is encrypted and wallet directly connects to cryptocurrency nodes. Wallet integrates decentralized mixer and atomic swaps and supports BTC, Ergo and USDT Omni.
