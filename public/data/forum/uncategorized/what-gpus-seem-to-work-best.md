### Post #3 — @kushti (2020-09-25)

From BigEvilSoloMiner on Discord:

"All the V2 hashrates are identical to V1 hashrates because there are no more free memory transactions for high capacity GPUs with V2 by duplicating the lookup table.

All GPUs were memory overclocked and energy efficiency may be improved with better implementation and tuning:

RTX 3080: ~200 MH/s @ 212W  
RTX 2080 Ti: ~94 MH/s @ 175W  
GTX 1080 Ti: ~70 MH/s @ 165W

Radeon VII: ~176 MH/s @ 225W  
RX Vega 64: ~84 MH/s @ 225W"
