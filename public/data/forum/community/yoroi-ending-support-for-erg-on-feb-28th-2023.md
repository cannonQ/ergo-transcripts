### Post #1 — @william_emurgo (2023-01-25)

Greetings everyone. William from EMURGO. I have some unfortunate news I wanted to share with the group. Yoroi will be ending support for $ERG on February 28th, 2023. If you no longer have access to your recovery phrase, it is absolutely paramount that you move your $ERG to a compatible wallet before that date. Otherwise, you can restore your wallet in a compatible wallet using your seed. We apologize for any inconvenience this has caused, and we’d like to thank the ERGO Platform and all of its community. For more information, please see our official announcement.
