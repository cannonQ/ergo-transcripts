# Ergo PoW Blockchain - AMA - December 4th 17UTC

## Executive Summary

This AMA focused primarily on the successful launch of the USE stablecoin protocol, featuring discussions with developer kushti and project lead Richie about the community-driven launch that raised 395,000 ERG from 129 participants. The call also covered updates on Rosen Bridge expansion, Lithos testnet progress, and various ecosystem developments.

## Key Topics Discussed

• **USE Stablecoin Launch** - Community-driven algorithmic stablecoin launch raised 395,000 ERG with 129 participants
• **Cross-chain Expansion Strategy** - Plans to expand USE to other chains via Rosen Bridge, starting with Binance Smart Chain
• **Liquidity Efficiency** - Discussion of Uniswap v3 type pools offering 20x efficiency improvements for stablecoins
• **Machina Finance Integration** - Potential integration of USE with upcoming Machina Finance grid trading system
• **Privacy and Midnight Protocol** - Discussion of bringing USE to Midnight for private stablecoin functionality
• **Rosen Bridge Updates** - Runes support now live, enabling Bitcoin asset wrapping
• **Lithos Testnet Progress** - First successful fraud proof executed, high community participation in mining
• **Community Projects** - Updates on BRIC educational token experiment and new community hubs
• **Research Papers** - Multiple papers accepted for stability workshop in India (December 15th)

## Technical Concepts Explained

• **CLB Framework** - Community Liquidity Backing system allowing safe, low-risk community investment
• **Algorithmic Stablecoin Mechanics** - USE operates with ERG-backed reserves and automatic price stabilization
• **Concentrated Liquidity** - Uniswap v3 style pools provide 20x capital efficiency for stablecoin trading
• **Fraud Proofs in Lithos** - Miners audit each other's work and get rewarded for catching misrepresentations
• **Interactive Transaction Construction** - Using partially signed Bitcoin transactions for complex swaps
