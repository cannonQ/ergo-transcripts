# Ergo Community Call Summary - October 23rd, 2025

## Executive Summary
Community discussion focused on the recent Kadena blockchain collapse and broader "rug season" affecting centralized blockchain projects. The team discussed Ergo's resilience as a decentralized platform and potential opportunities to help affected communities transition to more sustainable blockchain models.

## Key Topics Discussed
• **Kadena Collapse Analysis** - Discussion of Kadena's corporate-controlled structure leading to its downfall
• **"Rug Season" Concept** - kushti's term for the current wave of centralized blockchain project failures
• **Centralized Exchange Risks** - Criticism of Binance and other centralized platforms as unsustainable
• **Community vs Corporate Blockchains** - Comparison between community-driven projects like Ergo and company-owned chains
• **Ergo Foundation Evolution** - Discussion of EF's planned transition away from central control
• **Infrastructure Updates** - Node 6 release and upcoming website redesign mentioned
• **Rosen Bridge Opportunities** - Potential to help displaced communities through cross-chain bridging
• **Long-term Vision** - Ergo positioned as a "cathedral" built to last centuries like Unix

## Notable Announcements or Decisions
• Node 6 has been released with plans for sub-blocks implementation
• New community website is ready for launch, designed with a "sci-fi punk" aesthetic
• Lithos testnet will need miners in November
• Video summaries from the Ergo meetup have been published with AI-generated bookmarks

## Technical Concepts Explained
• **Ship of Theseus Analogy** - Used to explain how healthy communities evolve while maintaining continuity
• **ASIC Resistance Benefits** - Discussed as potential solution for displaced mining communities
• **Trustless Pegging** - Explained in context of helping other blockchain communities

## Action Items or Follow-ups Mentioned
• Continue monitoring for actual community organization within Kadena user base
• Potential Twitter Space with Kadena community if they self-organize
• Launch new community website on infra infrastructure
• Offer ASIC-resistant mining transition path to affected communities
