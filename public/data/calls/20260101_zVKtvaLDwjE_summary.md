                # Ergo PoW Blockchain 2025: A Year of Real Progress | What Comes Next

## Executive Summary

This annual community call reviewed Ergo's significant progress in 2025, including the launch of ChainCash, Basis, multiple stablecoin projects (USE, Dexy Gold, Gluon), and cross-chain bridge expansion. The discussion covered upcoming developments for 2026 including subpools/sigma chains, fast blocks, and the transition to tail emission schedule, while addressing broader challenges in the cryptocurrency industry around building better decentralized money.

## Key Topics Discussed

• **ChainCash and Basis Development** - Multi-year monetary framework project reaching working prototype stage for off-chain payments with blockchain reserves
• **Stablecoin Ecosystem Growth** - USE stablecoin launched as algorithmic solution, Dexy Gold operational, Gluon USD in development
• **Subpools/Sigma Chains** - Months of DevNet testing complete, white paper nearly ready for public release
• **Rosen Bridge Expansion** - BSC integration successful, Bitcoin meta-protocol development, open contribution standards established
• **Lithos Mining Protocol** - Third testnet version releasing with fraud proofs, addressing proof-of-work centralization issues
• **Developer Ecosystem Growth** - New faces from hackathon, wallet updates, increased community project activity
• **Block Reward Schedule** - Approaching tail emission in April 2026 with 0.85% yearly inflation rate
• **Cross-chain Liquidity Strategy** - Focus on smaller ecosystems over crowded markets like Ethereum
• **Industry Critique** - Discussion of crypto industry's departure from fundamental money principles

## Technical Concepts Explained

• **Demurrage/Storage Rent** - Ergo's mechanism for long-term network security without infinite inflation
• **Merge Mining** - Ability for miners to secure multiple chains simultaneously for additional revenue
• **Algorithmic Stablecoins** - Decentralized stablecoins without centralized collateral or control
• **ChainCash Architecture** - Off-chain payment system with blockchain reserves for mesh network payments
• **Meta-protocols on Bitcoin** - Using Bitcoin network for asset movement without native smart contract support
