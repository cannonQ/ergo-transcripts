# Ergo General Chat — 2019-W02 Summary

## Key Topics Discussed

- **Ergo PoW Algorithm (Autolykos)**: Community discussing the choice of proof-of-work mechanism and its naming origin (derived from Autolycus). Dmitry confirmed more educational materials on PoW justification, smart contract language design, and fee model would arrive soon.

- **ErgoScript Development Challenge**: Dmitry offered 100 EFYT (~$200) bounty for community members to write original (non-copy-paste) ErgoScript examples. Challenge included references to existing Sigma protocol examples and smart contract blog posts as learning resources.

- **EFYT Token & Mainnet Swap**: Clarification that EFYT (Ergo First Year Token) is issued on Waves blockchain; swap to native ERG coins will occur after mainnet launch. Emission curve published showing total supply of 97,739,925 coins.

- **Mainnet Launch Timeline**: Multiple references to expected mainnet launch in late January 2019, with voting mechanism announced as live.

- **Ergo Data Chain**: Acknowledged as unplanned for pre-mainnet; decision to design after mainnet launch confirmed.

- **Sigma Protocol & Formal Specification**: References to sigma whitepaper in LaTeX format as specification for ErgoScript capabilities.

## Important Decisions or Announcements

- [@Dmitry, msg#5615, 2019-01-11]: Ergo emission curve published; total supply confirmed at 97,739,925 coins. Ergo Data chain deferred to post-mainnet phase.
