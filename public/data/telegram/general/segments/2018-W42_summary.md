## Key Topics Discussed

- **Privacy Implementation**: Ergo will feature Monero-like privacy but not identically. Privacy will be optional (not default) and implemented after Sigma-language completion. More complex schemes may follow.

- **Mainnet Launch Timeline**: Goal is mainnet by end of 2018, but more realistically Q1 2019 after Version 2.0 rollout and debugging. Version 1.7 was recently released.

- **Mining & Hash Rental**: Autolykos mining algorithm final implementation expected soon in testnet2. Users without strong hardware can rent hash from services like NiceHash and point to Ergo's chain at mainnet.

- **Tooling & Developer Contribution**: Smart contract testing, examples, and tutorials needed. Developers encouraged to contribute. Brand/logo undergoing finalization before mainnet merchandise.

- **Protocol Presentation**: Vasily Chepurnoy will present the formal protocol in a talk; formal paper not yet finished but author keen to publicize.
