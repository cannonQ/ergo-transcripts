# Ergo General Chat — 2017-W50 Summary

## Key Topics Discussed

- **TestNet Progress & Releases**: Version 0.1.2 released with syncing adjustments; version 0.1.3 released by end of week. Explorer available at http://ergo.dsalab.de/. Syncing issues identified and being resolved.

- **Mining Timeline**: GPU mining expected sometime in 2018 after TestNet fully debugged. Equihash algorithm confirmed. Open mining targeting early Q2 2018.

- **Token Supply & Economics**: Total supply 100 million ERG; 50 million maximum issuable in first year. EFYT (Waves-based token) trading on Waves DEX with 1:1 future conversion to ERG. Mining will generate additional 50 million coins to reach total supply.

- **Airdrop Activity**: Previous airdrop to Waves Community Token (WCT) holders confirmed. Future airdrops possible without advance notice, not limited to WCT holders.

- **Public Engagement**: Dmitry and Alexander presented at Primalbase Berlin Hub on 2017-12-15. Q&A session #3 planned before year-end.
