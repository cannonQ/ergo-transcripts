# Ergo General Chat — 2019-W21 Summary

## Key Topics Discussed

- **Mainnet Launch Date**: Ergo mainnet scheduled for June 20, 2019. Currently, Ergo exists as EFYT (Ergo First Year Token) on Waves platform; the independent blockchain will launch separately.

- **Token vs. Blockchain Clarification**: Community members clarified that only the token is currently on Waves; the actual Ergo blockchain is a separate endeavor launching independently from the Waves platform.

- **Charles Hoskinson Endorsement**: Charles Hoskinson referenced Ergo's "Proof of NO Premine" in a YouTube video, lending credibility to the project's approach to fair token distribution.

- **Wallet Integration Timeline**: Integration with Magnum wallet and other wallets will occur after mainnet launch. Until then, EFYT trading happens on Waves DEX.

- **Token Swap Mechanism**: At mainnet launch, users will swap EFYT to ERG on Waves, then withdraw ERG to personal wallets. A one-day delay exists between block minting and spending rewards.
