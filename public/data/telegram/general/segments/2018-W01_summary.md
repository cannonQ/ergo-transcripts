# Ergo General Chat — 2018-W01 Summary

## Key Topics Discussed

- **Proof-of-Work consensus and throughput scaling**: Discussion of Ergo's PoW foundation using Equihash, contrasted with emerging alternatives like Hashgraph. Bitcoin NG protocol cited as a first-layer scaling approach, with theoretical throughput up to 1,100 TPS. Waves referenced as early implementation achieving 100 TPS.

- **Token supply and economic model**: Circulating supply of ~400k tokens at time of discussion, with total supply of 100 million ERG planned post-launch. Mining reward structure outlined: roughly 50 million tokens estimated to be mined in year one, with 10% retained as team fee.

- **Swap mechanism from EFYT to ERG**: Conversion structure to be determined, with stated intention to reward early adopters. Swap mechanism to be handled through Waves platform; holders need only keep tokens in Waves wallet during conversion.

- **Market speculation and investor psychology**: Extended debate on market cap metrics, token valuation, and behavioral differences between cryptocurrency speculators and traditional investors. Discussion of XRP, Cardano, TRON valuations as case studies.
