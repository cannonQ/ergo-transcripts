# Ergo General Chat — 2018-W09 Summary

## Key Topics Discussed

- **EFYT to ERG Token Conversion**: EFYT tokens will convert to 2 separate Ergo tokens (ErgoMoney and ErgoData) at a 2:1 exchange ratio. Early buyers may not receive a premium, as the value of issued tokens is not predetermined and depends on market conditions at swap time.

- **Mainnet Launch Timeline**: Originally targeted for May 2018, but no exact date has been confirmed. A clearer roadmap and estimated launch date are expected by end of Q2 2018. TestNet began in January 2018.

- **Mining ETA**: Mining details remain unclear; roadmap clarity promised by end of Q2 2018.

- **Regulatory Positioning**: Ergo's non-ICO distribution (airdrop to WCT holders, planned IOHK employee distribution) positions it favorably against emerging SEC/CFTC regulatory scrutiny, particularly compared to projects that conducted public ICOs.
