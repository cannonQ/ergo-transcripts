# Ergo General Chat — 2020-W11 Summary
**Period:** 2020-03-09 to 2020-03-15  
**Message Count:** 322  
**Primary Activity:** Airdrop campaign management and project onboarding

---

## Key Topics Discussed

- **Airdrop Campaign (Magnum)**: Active distribution of ERG tokens through task completion. Rewards initially 0.7 ERG per participant, with 0.05 ERG for referrals. Community feedback indicated rewards were perceived as small relative to token value (~$0.12–$0.18 USD per ERG).

- **Token Exchange Listings**: Multiple participants requested information on exchange listings and partnerships. Ergo was confirmed live on CoinMarketCap and available on ProBit, Tidex, HotBit, and Waves.exchange.

- **Mining and Network Participation**: Discussion of mining pools (@ergo_mining channel referenced) as alternative to airdrop for acquiring ERG. No technical mining details provided in this segment.

- **Storage Rent Feature**: [@scalahub, msg#24519, 2020-03-12]: Highlighted storage rent as a distinctive Ergo feature requiring ongoing payment to maintain unspent coins — described as underemphasized in marketing.

- **Project Positioning**: Discussion of Ergo as "digital gold" alternative with emphasis on being "money" rather than purely speculative asset. Contrast drawn with Bitcoin's evolution toward banking settlement layer.
