# Ergo General Chat — 2025-W49 Summary

## Key Topics Discussed

- **USE Stablecoin Deployment**: kushti actively working on USE deployment with calculations and implementation details. ChainCash paper to be presented at Stability Workshop in Jaipur, India on December 15, 2025.

- **Subblocks Implementation**: Community discussion on prioritizing subblocks as critical infrastructure for medium of exchange. kushti noted 95% core code complete, needs review. Debate over whether subblocks should be priority over other optimizations.

- **Rosen Bridge & Cross-Chain Integration**: Firo partnership announced for decentralized cross-chain access to Ethereum, BNB Chain, and Cardano. Discussion on Rosen's role as double-layered coordination network over Ergo.

- **SilverCents & Street Assets Tokenization**: Grayman detailed SilverCents as hybrid currency for local circular economies using ChainCash protocol with constitutional silver collateralization. Discussion on "street assets tokenization" vs corporate RWA tokenization.

- **Chainlink & Palmyra Oracle Integration**: Proof-of-reserve functions coupled with IoT devices (cameras, weight sensors, motion sensors) for on-chain warehouse receipts and decentralized trade financing. Plan to build native Ergo oracles alongside Chainlink.

- **Privacy & Anon Development**: mx emphasized need for optional privacy schemes, stealth addresses, non-custodial/non-interactive design, and Autolykos v2. Discussion on combining Ergo's programmable privacy with Nym's mixnet.

- **Lithos Protocol v2.0**: Released with GPU mining optimization. Airdrop announced for mainnet tokens to testnet miners supporting the protocol.

- **Bene V2 Launch**: Multi-asset fundraising platform now live with support for ERG, SigmaUSD, GAU, and custom tokens. Client-side P2P architecture, no servers, integrated with Nautilus and SafeW wallets.

- **MewFinance Ecosystem**: CLB/USE liquidity pool launched on MewDex. Burn dApp (burn.mewfinance.com) for token burning with leaderboard rewards. Discussion on meme coin launches (FAKU, yo papá) for ecosystem engagement.

- **Spectrum DEX vs Order Books**: qx() advocated for Trade House (order book DEX) over Spectrum (AMM) for full transparency, depth visibility, and control over slippage. Discussed partial order fills and peer-to-peer trading capabilities.

- **Cake Wallet Integration**: mx criticized lack of Ergo integration on Cake Wallet; discussion on what differentiates Ergo's privacy scheme from false privacy advocates promoting "optional privacy" as standard feature.

- **Stability Workshop Papers**: Multiple peer-reviewed papers accepted for presentation:
  - Money Creation With Elastic Supply (kushti)
  - Djed Shu: Stablecoin Protocol with Two Oracles
  - Dexy: Algorithmic Central Bank Design (kushti & Luca)
  - Gluon W: Cryptocurrency Stabilization Protocol
  - Orb: Decentralized Oracles
  - Fate Protocol: Perpetual Prediction Pools
  - RainDrop: Reward Distribution Protocol

- **P2P Cash Philosophy**: kushti reframed Ergo's endgame as p2p economies and p2p money enabler via self-sovereign banks, agreement protocols (oracle pools, Rosen), trustless derivatives, trust-minimized tokenization, and offchain cash with credit creation for local/global economies.

- **Keystone Hardware Wallet Integration**: In review by Nautilus; legit concerns raised about external library dependencies. Expected December release or later.

- **CoinGecko/CoinMarketCap Updates**: Yulius systematically updating Ergo social links, adding sigmaspace.io and ergexplorer.com across multiple platforms. Blockspot.io updated with latest explorer links.

- **GPU Mining Longevity**: Discussion on hardware market effects. Armeanio noted ETH's transition to PoS eliminated GPU mining hardware wall and external economic impact globally. Grayman/mx concerned about centralized hardware advantage in future PoW variants.
