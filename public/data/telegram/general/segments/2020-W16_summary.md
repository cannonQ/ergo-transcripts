# Ergo General Chat Summary — 2020-W16
**Period:** 2020-04-13 to 2020-04-19  
**Activity Level:** Low technical content; primarily exchange listing inquiries and community support

---

## Key Topics Discussed

- **Exchange Listings & Liquidity**: Community members inquired about major exchange listings (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, Bittrex). Project listed on Waves.exchange, Tidex, Probit, and HotBit. Tidex identified as most liquid at the time. [@filippounits, msg#25748; @Unknown, msg#25908]

- **MarketCap Discrepancy**: CMC reported 1.7M vs CoinGecko's 2.2M. CMC incorrectly deducted treasury funds from market cap calculation, contrary to PoW coin standards like Zcash. [@Jason Davies, msg#25821]

- **Mining & Profitability**: Community members asked about hashrate, earnings per GPU (1080 Ti, 2080 Ti), and profit margins at different price points (0.00002 BTC). [@Unknown, msg#25825, msg#25949, msg#25952]

- **Airdrop Distribution**: Rewards distributed until 2020-04-27. [@filippounits, msg#25752; @Unknown, msg#25961]

- **Wallet Options**: Magnum and CoinBarn recommended; community interest in offline storage and secure wallets. [@filippounits, msg#25776; @Kookster, msg#25777]

- **Project Fundamentals**: Questions on funding, team size, project purpose (store of value vs. smart money platform), and ICO history. [@Cryptominded, msg#25805; @Christopher House, msg#25898; @Phantom, msg#25899]
