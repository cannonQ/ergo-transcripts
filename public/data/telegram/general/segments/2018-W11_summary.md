# Ergo General Chat — 2018-W11 Summary

## Key Topics Discussed

- **Token Supply & Economics**: Extensive community discussion on circulating vs. total supply, with evolving estimates ranging from 8–20M tokens (first year) to ~100M total. First-year emission projected at ~20M tokens across 8-year emission schedule.

- **Market Entry & Liquidity**: Community members discussing EFYT acquisition primarily via Waves DEX, with noted challenges around liquidity and pricing given low circulating supply (~400K at time of discussion).

- **Mainnet Timeline**: Confirmation that mainnet launch was targeted for later in 2018 (not May as initially speculated), with no planned ICO.

- **Mining as Distribution Mechanism**: PoW mining confirmed as the primary method for distributing remaining supply beyond initial circulating tokens.
