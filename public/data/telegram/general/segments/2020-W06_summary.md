# Ergo General Chat — 2020-W06 Summary

## Key Topics Discussed

- **DeFi Strategy & Vision**: Discussion of Ergo's approach to decentralized finance, with comparisons to THORChain and Kava. Emphasis on Ergo's potential for truly peer-to-peer contracts and non-interactive approaches, contrasting with centralized DeFi alternatives. [@Unknown, msg#16674]

- **Privacy-Preserving Voting with Sigma Protocols**: Technical exploration of community voting schemes using Sigma protocols with El-Gamal encryption for minimal on-chain load and privacy preservation. [@Unknown, msg#16671]

- **Mining Pools & WhatToMine Listing**: Ergo was not yet listed on WhatToMine; pool launch planned for approximately one week after testnet launch in early February 2020. [@MHS_SAM, msg#16670, msg#16672]

- **Circulating Supply Discrepancy**: CMC data was showing incorrect circulating supply; real cap estimated between 5.5–6 million ERG with large miners holding rather than dumping. [@Unknown, msg#16741, msg#16742, msg#16743]

- **HotBit Withdrawal Delays**: Multiple users experienced ERG withdrawal verification delays on HotBit exchange, resolved through support tickets. [@filippounits, msg#16682, msg#16697]

## Important Decisions or Announcements

- [@MHS_SAM, msg#16670, 2020-02-04T02:17]: Testnet launch planned for the week of February 3–9, 2020, with pool launch expected one week after.

- [@Unknown, msg#16673, 2020-02-04T02:51]: Privacy-preserving voting scheme flagged as candidate for 2021 roadmap.

## Technical Q&A Worth Preserving

- **Q** (@Unknown): "Another gap I'm seeing is interaction between smart contracts and the real world. A smart contract cannot privately store a message then send that message to an email."
  **A** (@Unknown): Oracle bridges like Chainlink are valued for solving this gap; implication that Ergo would need similar mechanisms for real-world data integration. [msg#16662, msg#16665]

- **Q** (@Unknown): How does privacy-preserving community voting work in Ergo?
  **A** (@Unknown): Using Sigma protocols with El-Gamal encryption: "voting outcome is a value encrypted with El-Gamal scheme... we have minimal on-chain load, privacy-preserving voting and money moved via (possibly large-scale) community voting." [msg#16671]
