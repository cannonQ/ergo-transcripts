# Ergo General Chat — 2019-W34 Summary

## Key Topics Discussed

- **Exchange Listings & Trading:** Community discussion around Bisq listing, Waves DEX trading, and exploration of centralized exchange options (Binance listing fees mentioned as potentially prohibitive ~500 BTC). ERG/EFYT swap execution times queried.

- **Mining & Hardware:** GPU hashrate inquiries for Nvidia cards (1070 ~20 MH/s, 1080 Ti ~36 MH/s). Discussion of decentralized mining constraints noting that Ergo mining pools require trust relationships between miners, making traditional pools unlikely.

- **Community Fundraising Models:** Exploration of crowdfunding mechanisms including T-shirt merchandise, bounty programs, and a proposed ERG-based grants platform modeled on ZF Grants but using smart contracts instead of centralized escrow.

- **Hardware Wallet Integration:** Investigation into Trezor and Ledger integration complexity, with preference noted for Trezor's security model. Wrapped BTC (similar to WBTC) proposed as a potential mechanism for cross-chain funding.

- **Ergo Smart Contract Grants Platform:** Detailed proposal for a decentralized crowdfunding website using ErgoScript contracts (P2S addresses) for each proposal, with automatic refund mechanisms if funding goals not met within time limits.
