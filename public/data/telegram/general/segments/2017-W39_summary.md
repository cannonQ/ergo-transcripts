**Period:** 2017-09-30 to 2017-11-01  
**Messages Analyzed:** 56  
**Participants:** 8

## Key Topics Discussed

- **Ergo Independence & IOHK Relationship**: Clarification that Ergo is a totally independent project collaborating with IOHK only in research, despite kushti being listed on IOHK's team.

- **Testnet Launch Timeline**: Public testnet release was imminent but no exact date was communicated. Mining on testnet was explicitly not yet enabled.

- **Tokenomics & Supply Model**: First-year supply capped at ~50M ERG (potentially reduced), with total supply not to exceed 100M. Inflation curve designed to issue coins fast (within 5 years) while storage rent mechanism would return lost coins to circulation for ongoing miner rewards.

- **Proof of Work & Mining**: Ergo will use proof of work (not PoS) with Equihash algorithm. Dedicated hardware will eventually be required, though specifics were under development.

## Technical Q&A Worth Preserving

- **Q** (@Unknown, msg#186): Can I mine the coin in testnet0?  
  **A** (@Terri, msg#187): Not yet. The team will make an announcement when mining becomes possible.

- **Q** (@Albert @ Genlayer, msg#222): If first-year supply is 100M and price is ~$10, won't 10x inflation in year one crash the price?  
  **A** (@Dmitry, msg#239-#241): The tokenomics model is designed so that even after fast initial issuance, storage rent will return lost coins to circulation and provide sufficient miner rewards in the long term to maintain decentralization without price collapse. Economy model was still under construction.

- **Q** (@Andrey Kalinchev, msg#246): When can I mine Ergo and in what script?  
  **A** (@Terri, msg#247): Coming soon, using Equihash.

- **Q** (@Albert @ Genlayer, msg#240): Won't concentrating mining only in the first years lead to centralization?  
  **A** (@Dmitry, msg#241): The long-term design goal is to ensure miner rewards remain sufficient after the initial period to support a decentralized network. The economy model was still under construction at the time.
