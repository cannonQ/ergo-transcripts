## Key Topics Discussed

- **Mining profitability & market dynamics:** Discussion of miner behavior, large-scale coin dumping (50t daily), and impact on price. Noted that early mainnet miners accumulated significant holdings and potential whale manipulation via price suppression.

- **Airdrop distribution:** Multiple inquiries about airdrop timing and mechanics. Community members asking about when distribution would occur and how bounty hunters/airdrop recipients factor into selling pressure.

- **Exchange listing strategy:** @Vadim V (msg#21964, msg#21967) advised that large institutional buyer accumulation ($300t+/month) and Binance listing partnerships are required to support price; mining supply flooding market daily requires coordinated large-fund buying.

- **Pool operations & GPU mining:** Questions about mining profitability with mid-range GPUs (GTX 1080), pool access, and technical setup. Pool operator referenced as primarily active on Discord.

- **Community marketing:** @bez (msg#20392, msg#21732) called for coordinated social media promotion across Twitter and Reddit via community-driven marketing channel.
