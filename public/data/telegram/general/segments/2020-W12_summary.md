# Ergo General Chat — Week 2020-W12 Summary

## Key Topics Discussed

- **Ergo Platform Use Cases & Value Proposition**: Multiple posts outlined five core use cases: digital gold via proof-of-work scarcity, privacy-preserving applications (next-generation mixer in development), safe and simple financial contracts with complex signature schemes, alternative financial systems (targeted loans, local exchange trading), and money for hard times (light client support, digital gold properties for regions with limited internet or failing institutions).

- **Airdrop Distribution Timeline**: Airdrop rewards distribution window confirmed as through 27 April 2020 to participating ERGO wallets.

- **Exchange Listings & Liquidity**: Discussion of available trading venues with most liquidity noted as ProBit, Tidex, HotBit, and Waves.exchange. Community questions raised about listings on major exchanges (Binance, Bittrex) and market ranking concerns.

- **Mining Pools & Earning ERG**: References to mining as a method to obtain ERG, with direction to dedicated mining channel (@ergo_mining) and pool options.

- **Sigma Protocols Research**: Publication of technical article on Sigma protocols as part of "Building Ergo" series.
