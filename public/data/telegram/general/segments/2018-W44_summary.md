# Ergo General Chat Summary — 2018-W44
**Period:** 2018-10-29 to 2018-11-11  
**Activity Level:** Low-to-moderate; primarily community Q&A and announcement sharing

---

## Key Topics Discussed

- **Proof of Work Algorithm & Mining**  
  PoW code not yet finalized. GPU, ASIC, and CPU mining all planned; traditional mining pools being phased out. Detailed specifications and algorithm paper to be released after November presentation.

- **First-Year Emission Curve**  
  Community clarified that first-year emission is 19.7 million ERG (not 50 million as misreported on Bitcointalk). This maintains reasonable inflation and market cap growth trajectory comparable to Cardano and Zcash at similar stages.

- **Mainnet Launch Timeline**  
  Target end of 2018 or Q1 2019. TestNet progress is the primary indicator for mainnet readiness; no fixed date announced.

- **EFYT Token Exchange**  
  EFYT (pre-mainnet token) currently available only on Waves Exchange. Will be exchangeable for mined ERG only after mainnet launch.

- **Documentation & Localization**  
  Community feedback flagged need for English proofreading of front-facing docs and materials before public mainnet launch, given non-English-speaking core team.
