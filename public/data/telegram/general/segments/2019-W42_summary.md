# Ergo General Chat — 2019-W42 Summary

## Key Topics Discussed

- **DEX Architecture & Design**: Discussion of decentralized exchange models, specifically order book vs. AMM approaches and challenges with on/off-ramps for cross-chain gateways [@Jason Davies, msg#14281].

- **Exchange Integration & Wallet Support**: Multiple issues reported with Hotbit deposit/withdrawal functionality affecting users; discussions around wallet options (Magnum, Waves DEX, Ledger support planned) for storing ERG [@KJSDNGlksdnnds, msg#14427-14432].
