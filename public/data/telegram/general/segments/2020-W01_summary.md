# Ergo General Chat — 2020-W01 Summary

## Key Topics Discussed

- **Exchange Listing Strategy**: Extended discussion about targeting Bittrex, Poloniex, OKEX, Bitfinex, Kraken, and Huobi. Debate over listing fees, regulatory compliance, and the distinction between reported vs. actual trading volumes on CoinMarketCap vs. CoinGecko. [@filippounits, @Dmitry, @Unknown across msgs #16058–#16219]

- **EFYT Token Swap Progress**: Ergo Foundation YieldToken (EFYT) swap from Waves platform to native ERG coins at 1:1 ratio ongoing, with weak demand noted. [@Unknown, msg#16056, msg#16057, msg#16045–#16047]

- **Academic-to-Production Pipeline**: Recognition that Ergo's approach integrates published research directly into live code, reversing the typical pattern where academic work diverges from implementation. [@Unknown, msg#16022; @scalahub, msg#16023]

- **Supply Milestone**: 10M ERG supply reached. [@Unknown, msg#16052]

- **Bisq DEX Support**: Technical discussion of BSQ fee requirements for reactivating inactive trading pairs. [@filippounits, msg#16228–#16229; @Jason Davies, msg#16236–#16237]

- **Wallet Implementation**: Request for simple, user-friendly native wallet (Bitcoin Core model). Coinbarn mentioned as existing option. [@Andrew, msg#16049; @Kookster, msg#16050]

## Unresolved Questions

- **Exchange Selection Priority**: No consensus reached on which single "top-tier" exchange to prioritize. Community debated tradeoffs between Bittrex (solid but low volume), Poloniex (high volume but Justin Sun acquisition concerns), OKEX, Kraken, and others. [@Unknown–@Dmitry, msgs #16104–#16160]

- **Regulatory Compliance Costs**: Uncertainty around actual legal/documentation fees required by different exchanges for listing approval. [@Unknown, msg#16210]

- **DEX Timeline**: When Ergo will have native DEX infrastructure competitive with centralized exchanges remains unclear; community flagged this as medium-term necessity. [@Unknown, msg#16210]

- **GUI/UX Priorities**: Whether core team should allocate resources to GUI/wallet UX improvements flagged as gap, but no decision documented. [@Unknown, msg#16195–#16196]
