**Period:** 2018-05-03 to 2018-05-12  
**Participants:** 7  
**Messages analyzed:** 58

## Key Topics Discussed

- **Ergo vs. Cardano relationship**: Clarification that Ergo founders have collaborated with IOHK on research but do not write code for Cardano and do not use Cardano's codebase. Ergo is a separate project with its own vision, built from scratch.

- **ASIC mining and Bitmain Z9**: Discussion of Bitmain's announcement of an Equihash ASIC miner (Z9). Community concern raised about centralization and exclusion of small miners. Noted that Ergo's focus on simplicity may preclude frequent hard-fork anti-ASIC strategies like Monero employs.

- **Ergo protocol architecture**: Confirmation that Ergo is built from scratch using Scorex framework. Emphasis on published research papers and the protocol's differentiation from existing blockchains.

- **Project maturity and content availability**: At this stage (May 2018), no YouTube channel exists; community directed to website (ergoplatform.org) for technical documentation, TECH section, and FAQ.

- **Authenticated dynamic dictionaries (in papers)**: Question raised about the meaning of "authenticated dynamic dictionary" terminology in Ergo whitepapers; no response provided in this segment.

- **EFYT trading on Waves DEX**: Clarification that EFYT (pre-MainNet Ergo token) trades only on Waves DEX; mainchain swap and broader exchange availability expected post-MainNet launch.
